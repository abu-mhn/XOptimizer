What's New

Recent changes to X Optimizer.

  • Share button. A Share button in the tournament toolbar copies (or hands off to your phone's share sheet) a direct link to the Tournament tab.
  • Hosts and co-hosts can play too. Tap "+ Register myself" in the registering screen to add yourself as a participant.
  • Past tournaments stay viewable. Reset clears the live room but keeps a snapshot — clicking the entry in history still shows the final results.
  • Each tab has its own URL. /calculator/, /deck/, /tournament/, etc. — bookmark or share a direct link to any tab.
  • Scoreboard round counter. Shows the current round above VS, advancing every 3 score taps so judges always know where they are.
