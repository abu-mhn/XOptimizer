What's New

Recent changes to X Optimizer.

Version v1.4.1

  • Sign in or play as Guest. Joining as Participant now asks — sign in to earn ranking points, or Become Guest to play without affecting the leaderboard.
  • Auto-rejoin as host. Sign in on any device and you're dropped straight back into the tournaments you host.
  • Sign out leaves the room. Signing out while inside a tournament returns you to the Open Tournaments lobby; the tournament itself stays live for everyone else.
  • Tester tag. The bulk-add Test button is now hidden unless your account is tagged "Tester".
