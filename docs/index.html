What's New

Recent changes to X Optimizer.

Version v1.2.6

  • Decks sync across devices. Sign in to your account and the decks you build follow you — save on your phone, see them on your laptop. Edits propagate live across signed-in devices.
  • Host accounts. Hosting a tournament now needs a free email account. Sign up / sign in / forgot password are all built into the popup; the same account works on every device.
  • Account row in Settings. Settings → Account shows your email when signed in, with a Sign out button. Sign in from there or get prompted automatically when you tap Create Tournament.
  • Rooms survive backgrounding. Hosts who switch to another app mid-registration no longer have their tournament disappear from the lobby — it stays visible for participants until you click Start or Reset.
