What's New

Recent changes to X Optimizer.

Version v1.2.7

  • Dashboard tab. A new home tab with an auto-sliding carousel for Combo of the Day, Heaviest Bey, and Max ATK / DEF / STA — combo names are computed exactly like the calculator (e.g. BULLETGRIFFONY). Tap any part image to zoom it.
  • Best Parts cards. Below the combos, a second carousel shows the most-used parts in the current tournament, split by type (Blade, Lock Chip, Ratchet, Bit, etc.) with 1st (gold) / 2nd (silver) / 3rd (bronze) ranks. Results persist across tournament resets.
  • Revox tab. Revox Members now lives in its own top-level tab, with its own icon — no more digging into a Tournament sub-tab.
  • Parts Usage carousel. The pie charts shown after a finished tournament now auto-slide through each part type, so you can flip through them on mobile without scrolling a long page.
  • Tab bar improvements. Tabs sit in a single horizontal scroller (no visible scrollbar) and the scroll position is preserved when you switch tabs — no more jumping back to the start.
  • Share message polish. Tournament share text now formats the date as 14 May 2026 (Thursday) and adds a step pointing participants at the Deck tab's Copy → Paste flow during registration.
  • Deck paste fix. Pasting a CX or CX Expand deck into a tournament registration popup now keeps all of its parts (lock chip / main blade / assist blade) instead of dropping them.
  • Calculator parity in Dashboard. Best-bey calculations respect Bullet Griffon's built-in ratchet and Clock Mirage's -5 ratchet requirement, matching what the calculator allows.
