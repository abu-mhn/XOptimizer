What's New

Recent changes to X Optimizer.

Version v1.10.0

  • Battle Royale points are now monthly. Challenge another player to a points duel — you can only challenge someone with the same points or fewer; both stake an equal wager, you pick a Judge to oversee it, and the Judge declares the winner, who takes the pot. Points are no longer minted per tournament: instead your tournament ranking builds up all month, then at the turn of each month it's converted into Battle Royale points and the ranking resets to zero for a fresh race. Your BR balance and winnings always carry over.
  • Battle Royale: Battle & Shop tabs. The Battle Royale page now splits into a Battle tab (challenge players and settle duels) and a Shop tab (coming soon — spend your points). Battle Royale is now a signed-in feature, so the tab only appears once you log in.
  • Part pictures in the Calculator. Every Blade, Ratchet, Bit and CX part now shows its image right in the selection dropdown, laid out as a thumbnail grid so you can recognise parts at a glance instead of reading names.
  • Clock Mirage ratchet rule. Picking Clock Mirage now limits the ratchet list to "…5" ratchets and drops the "No Ratchet" option, matching how the blade is actually built.
  • Open or Closed tournaments. When you create a tournament you now choose its access. Open lists it publicly in the lobby; Closed keeps it private — it shows a "Closed" chip and asks for the room code when someone taps it. The host gets a copyable join code to share.
  • New blades + ratchet-less blade support. Fresh blades joined the roster — including Glory Valkyrie, Storm Pegasis and Heavens Ring. Blades flagged expandCX build with just a blade + bit (no ratchet, regular bit only), the same way Bullet Griffon works.
  • Participant limits + fee tracking + roles. Cap how many can register (auto-closes when full, shown in the lobby); a new Keeper role marks who's paid; and a Guest Judge role can co-host but not create tournaments.
