Recent changes to X Optimizer.

Version v1.2.9

  • Test button. Bulk-add synthetic participants with meta-random decks (weighted: ~75% Standard, ~13% CX, ~12% CX Expand). No parts repeat in a deck.
  • Best Parts polish. Multi-mode parts (Eclipse, Dual, Turbo, etc.) now show their image, and the 1st / 2nd / 3rd ranks stay aligned even when an asset is missing.
  • Bey Check cleaner. Slot labels are just "Slot 1 / 2 / 3", parts row scrolls without a visible scrollbar.
  • Carousels pause on touch. Auto-advance stops when you interact and resumes after 3 seconds idle.
  • Theme pass. Scoreboard, tournament registering view, match cards, Bey Check popup, and the Rounds / Groups pickers now follow every theme.
