What's New

Recent changes to X Optimizer.

Version v1.11.0

  • Calculator: one form, no sub-tabs. The BX/UX, CX and CX Expand sub-tabs are gone — you now switch build types straight from the dropdowns. In the Blade dropdown pick Split (Lock Chip + Main Blade + Assist Blade) for a custom (CX) build; from there the Main Blade's Split (Over Blade + Metal Blade) takes you to CX Expand. Each step has a Revert to go back.
  • Combine & Split parts. Combine (Blade + Ratchet) builds ratchet-less blades (Bullet Griffon, Glory Valkyrie) and shows only those blades; Combine (Ratchet + Bit) uses ratchet-bits and hides the ratchet slot. Both add a Revert to un-combine, and your existing picks (lock chip, assist blade, etc.) carry over when you switch.
  • Smarter Reset & Random. Reset always returns you to the basic BX/UX line, and Random can now roll any line — BX, CX or CX Expand — not just basic.
  • Part pictures in the Calculator. Every part shows its image right in the dropdown, as a thumbnail grid (up to 5 across on desktop) so you can recognise parts at a glance instead of reading names.
  • Clock Mirage ratchet rule. Picking Clock Mirage limits the ratchet list to "…5" ratchets, matching how the blade is actually built.
  • Battle Royale points are now monthly. Your tournament ranking builds up all month, then converts into Battle Royale points and resets for a fresh race. Challenge a player with the same points or fewer, stake an equal wager, and a Judge declares the winner — who takes the pot. Your BR balance always carries over.
  • Battle Royale: Battle & Shop tabs. The page splits into a Battle tab and a Shop tab (coming soon — spend your points). Battle Royale is a signed-in feature, so the tab appears once you log in.
