What's New

Recent changes to X Optimizer.

Version v1.5.9

  • New achievement — Paleonerd. Win 100 matches where every slot carries a Tyranno / Tricera / Ptera / Mammoth or Brachio part. Unlocks the Paleonerd title and an amber-jungle theme.
  • Two achievement renames. "Lonewolf" is now "Lone Wolf" and "King of Jungle" is now "King of The Jungle" — same conditions, cleaner titles.
  • Native share for tournaments. The Share Tournament popup now opens your phone's OS share sheet (WhatsApp, Discord, Messages, etc.) instead of just copying. Desktop browsers without native share still get the clipboard copy. Cancel and Share buttons gained icons to match the rest of the toolbar.
