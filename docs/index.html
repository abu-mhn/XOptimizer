What's New

Recent changes to X Optimizer.

Version v1.3.9

  • Combined blade view. CX and CX Expand combos now show the lock chip, blades and assist blade as one assembled image — tap it to slide through the parts.
  • Edit deck combos. Tap the pencil on any deck slot to rebuild that combo — swap parts or switch its mode.
  • Share as an image. New Share buttons on the Deck tab and calculator results.
  • Paste a deck. Drop a copied deck straight into the Deck tab with the new Paste button.
